## CONVERSION OPTIMIZATION

### Metrics to Track (Week 1-4)

Monitor these in Google Analytics:

- **Visitors to site** (total unique visitors)
- **Conversion rate to waitlist** (signups รท visitors)
  - Target: 10-20% conversion rate
- **Traffic sources** (where people find you)
  - Facebook, Reddit, Email, Direct, etc.
- **Bounce rate** (% who leave immediately)
  - Target: <50% bounce rate
- **Time on page** (average engagement)
  - Target: >2 minutes average

### A/B Tests (After 100+ Signups)

Test these elements to improve conversion:

- [ ] **Different headlines**
  - Current: "Choose Your Own Adventure Training Plans"
  - Test: "Training Plans That Adapt to Your Life"
  - Test: "Finally, Flexible Training Plans for Cross-Training Runners"

- [ ] **CTA button colors/text**
  - Current: "Join Beta Waitlist" (cyan)

  - Test: "Start Free Beta" (green)

- [ ] **Hero section design**
  - Current: Gray gradient background

  - Test: Action shot of runner on ElliptiGO

- [ ] **Feature description length**
  - Current: Short, punchy bullets
